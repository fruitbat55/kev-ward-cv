KEVWARDCV.CO.UK - WEBSITE SETUP GUIDE
========================================

Congratulations on purchasing kevwardcv.co.uk! Here's your step-by-step guide:

1. CHOOSE WEB HOSTING
--------------------
You need a hosting provider to store your website files.

Popular UK Hosting Options:
• SiteGround - Great performance, good support
• Krystal Hosting - UK-based, excellent support  
• TSO Host - UK company, affordable plans
• 123-reg - Often bundled with domain purchases
• Namecheap - Reliable and affordable
• Hostinger - Budget-friendly option

What to look for:
• cPanel or easy file management
• PHP support (for future features)
• SSL certificate included
• UK-based servers (faster for UK visitors)
• Around £3-8/month for basic shared hosting

2. POINT YOUR DOMAIN TO HOSTING
-------------------------------
Once you have hosting:
• Get nameservers from your hosting provider
• Log into your domain registrar account
• Update DNS/nameservers to point to your hosting

3. UPLOAD YOUR WEBSITE FILES
----------------------------
• Use FTP client (FileZilla - free) or hosting File Manager
• Upload all your files (.html, .css, images folder)
• Make sure index.html is in the root directory

4. TEST YOUR WEBSITE
-------------------
• Visit kevwardcv.co.uk to see if it loads
• Check all pages and links work
• Test on mobile devices

5. ADD PROFESSIONAL TOUCHES
---------------------------
• Set up professional email (kev@kevwardcv.co.uk)
• Add Google Analytics for visitor tracking
• Consider contact form instead of just email link
